Welcome to Husky Running Club!

Who are we?

Husky Running Club is a student organization at the University of Washington devoted to running. Neither membership nor previous running experience is required. Everyone is welcome! You can learn more about us here.

All runs leave from the quad at 5pm.

Weekly Schedule

  • Monday: 3/5/7 Mile Group Run
  • Tuesday: Track Workout
  • Wednesday: No Workout
  • Thursday: 3/5/7 Mile Group Run
  • Friday: Hill Workout or Long Run
